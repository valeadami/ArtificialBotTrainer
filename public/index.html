The Artificial Bot Trainer

The Artificial Bot Trainer is a chatbot created with Dialogflow ES.
It is a robot, like Robbie or Johnny 5. Perpetually hungry for data, it enjoys helping conversation designers and linguist with the bot training process. It will be happy to find variants for a sentence.
Try it! It will amaze you!
To get started, click on the popup at the bottom right of the page and go with the flow!
